Laguna Beach - Coastal Contemporary

Coastal Contemporary gem sitting on the bluffs of Laguna Beach. Designed by the renowned Horst Noppenberger. This house is the epitome of luxury coastal living, with exposed Steal Beams, floor to ceiling windows and doors showcasing unobstructed ocean views from all three levels.
