Luis Donan

Managing Partner

A visionary leader at the forefront of luxury custom construction, Luis Donan is a second-generation General Contractor whose journey has been rooted in a profound passion for creating exceptional living spaces. Guided by the wisdom passed down by his father, Luis’s formative years were a hands-on apprenticeship, as he honed his craft while working on diverse projects. Transitioning from the corporate landscape of luxury production homes, Luis’s pursuit of excellence led him to the realm of high-end custom builds, where he became the driving force behind Regal Construction’s inception.

With an innate understanding of design intricacies and a comprehensive grasp of Value Engineering, Luis ensures that every project is a masterclass in innovation and sophistication. His commitment to elevating the industry is mirrored in Regal’s philosophy, which values thorough planning, genuine collaboration, and a seamless fusion of timeless design and modern techniques.

Luis Donan doesn’t just build homes; he crafts legacies, transforming dreams into tangible works of art that stand as evidence of his unwavering dedication to luxury living.
